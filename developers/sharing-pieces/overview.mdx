## Methods
- [Contribute Back](/developers/sharing-addons/contribute): Publish your addon by contributing back your addon to main repository.
- [Community](/developers/sharing-addons/community): Publish your addon on npm directly and share it with the community.
- [Private](/developers/sharing-addons/private): Publish your addon on NRAI privately.
